Exam Conventions

The following conventions are used in this exam.

  1. The frame of reference of any problem is inertial unless otherwise stated.
  2. The direction of current is the direction in which positive charges would drift.
  3. The electric potential is zero at an infinite distance from an isolated point charge.
  4. All batteries and meters are ideal unless otherwise stated.
  5. Edge effects for the electric field of a parallel plate capacitor are negligible unless otherwise stated.
